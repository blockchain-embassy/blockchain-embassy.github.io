Coworking

The Blockchain Embassy is different from your average coworking environment.
We're not a serviced office - we're a community space.

  • Request Membership

175€ Membership

The shared office space has one type of membership only. For 175€ a month (excl. VAT), you can enjoy flexible smart lock access to the space during opening hours. No hassle and no strings attached. Join the community life at the Blockchain Embassy and get a variety of membership perks, such as free coffee, access to our podcast studio, online meeting room booking calendar, etc. We also host open meetups every month and invite our members for weekly crypto-breakfasts. There are no additional costs associated with a membership.
